# Sentinel → DoseAtlas Rebrand

- **Product name:** DoseAtlas (capital D, capital A, camelCase)
- **AI Persona:** Nurse Clippy (always capitalize)
- **Boilerplate:** "DoseAtlas turns your nursing study guides, drug labels, and clinical documents into a medication knowledge map — with Nurse Clippy as your guide."

- **Color palette:** Rose accent #fb7185, Coral accent #e07d5f, dark bg #0e0f0d, surface #1a1c18
- **Typography:** Inter font
