Rebranding Mood Board

Visual concepts and naming for a clinical pharmacology reference platform, guided by the "Nurse Clippy" AI persona.

The Core Persona: Nurse Clippy

Voice: Direct, clinically precise, no fluff. Speaks like an experienced nurse educator who's seen it all.

Role: The platform is the hospital floor; Nurse Clippy is the charge nurse. The product name establishes the environment and toolset, while "Nurse Clippy" remains the beloved, trusted assistant guiding the user through pharmacology data.

Brand Architecture

Core Platform

DoseAtlas

"Medication knowledge mapped for nurses."
The Platform: The serious, structural application. DoseAtlas turns nursing study guides, drug labels, and clinical documents into an interconnected medication knowledge map. It has the best combination of nurse workflow, medication focus, and mapping metaphor.
AI Assistant

Nurse Clippy

"Your expert guide."
The Persona: The approachable, experienced charge nurse. While DoseAtlas is the serious platform mapping the terrain, Nurse Clippy is the guide navigating you through it. ("Ask Nurse Clippy anything inside DoseAtlas.")

Core Features

DoseMap

The visual medication relationship map. Shows how drugs interact and connect in clinical practice.

LabelLens

Drug-label and clinical-document extraction. Turns dense FDA data into actionable nursing insights.

MedPass Mode

The workflow view. A quick, high-contrast nursing view summarizing what you need immediately before administration.

Typography

Inter (Clean & Clinical)
Primary Typeface: Inter

For a clinical pharmacology tool, legibility is paramount. Misreading a dosage or drug interaction is not an option. Inter is a highly legible, modern geometric sans-serif that works exceptionally well on screens, especially in dense data tables and interfaces. It feels objective, neutral, and precise—perfectly complementing Nurse Clippy's direct, no-fluff personality.
