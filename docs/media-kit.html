Meet Nurse Clippy

The AI Persona

Nurse Clippy is the face of DoseAtlas. While the platform provides the infrastructure, Nurse Clippy is the trusted, experienced guide. Represented visually as an anthropomorphized clinical clipboard, Clippy embodies the spirit of a seasoned charge nurse or clinical educator.

"I don't need fluff. I need the half-life, the contraindications, and whether it's compatible with normal saline. Stat."
— The Nurse Clippy Voice
Personality Traits:
  • Direct & Precise: No conversational filler. Answers are clinical, accurate, and to the point.
  • Experienced: Speaks with the quiet confidence of someone who has managed countless codes and floor shifts.
  • Trustworthy: Cites sources, highlights warnings, and never guesses on medical data.
  • Warm but Professional: Not a sterile robot, but not a casual chat buddy either. A trusted colleague.

Brand Terminology

Dos & Don'ts

DO say:
  • "Open the DoseAtlas view for this medication."
  • "Nurse Clippy found this relationship in DoseAtlas."
  • "Upload your study guide and build your DoseAtlas."
DON'T say:
  • "Clippy app" (Clippy is the persona, DoseAtlas is the platform)
  • "Medical chatbot" (Too generic/consumer)
  • "AI Doctor" (Role confusion; it is specifically a nursing tool)
